GOOGLE Cr-48 CHROMEBOOK WIKI


The Cr-48 is an unbranded notebook computer designed by Google and manufactured by Inventec. The Cr-48 was distributed by Google between December 2010 and April 2011 to participants in the Chrome OS Pilot Program.

Intended as a reference hardware design, these computers were not made available for sale to the general public. Approximately 60,000 Cr-48 notebooks were manufactured.

The guides provided here represent the distillation of work by numerous individuals. Original sources of information are noted in each guide.
